The Work of the Spirit

The Fruit of the Spirit

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control. Against such things there is no law."

— Galatians 5:22-23
L

Love

Selfless, sacrificial affection for others.
J

Joy

A deep sense of well-being that transcends circumstance.
P

Peace

Tranquility of heart derived from trust in God.
P

Patience

The capacity to accept or tolerate delay, trouble, or suffering without getting angry or upset.
K

Kindness

The quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate.
G

Goodness

The quality of being morally good or virtuous.
F

Faithfulness

Unwavering commitment and loyalty.
G

Gentleness

The quality of being kind, tender, or mild-mannered.
S

Self-control

The ability to control oneself, in particular one's emotions and desires.
