What are the Fruit of the Spirit?

love joy peace patience kindness goodness faithfulness gentleness self-control

"The fruit of the Spirit is the visible proof of the Holy Spirit's invisible presence in a believer's life."

In this clear and concise explanation, GotQuestions.org breaks down the meaning of the “Fruit of the Spirit” found in Galatians 5. They explain that this “fruit” is singular—representing a unified character—and is the direct result of the Holy Spirit’s work within a believer, contrasting sharply with the “works of the flesh.”

Key Takeaways

  • Supernatural Source: We cannot manufacture these virtues; they are produced by the Spirit.
  • Unified Character: It is one fruit with nine flavors, reflecting the fullness of Christ.
  • Active Transformation: The Spirit empowers us to overcome our natural sinful tendencies.
